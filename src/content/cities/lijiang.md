## Details
Lijiang Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage site with winding canals, traditional Naxi architecture, and views of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. It's a center of Naxi culture and Dongba religion.

## Physical Geography
Lijiang sits at 2,400 meters in northwestern Yunnan, with Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (5,596m) looming nearby. The high altitude brings cool temperatures and intense sun. The old town is built around a network of canals fed by mountain springs.

## Political Context
Lijiang is a prefecture-level city in Yunnan Province. The old town's UNESCO status (1997) has brought tourism wealth but also challenges in maintaining authenticity and managing overtourism.

## People & Culture
The Naxi people are the indigenous ethnic group, known for their matriarchal traditions and unique Dongba pictographic script. Tourism has transformed the economy, with many locals running guesthouses and shops in the old town.
