## Details
Chongqing is famous for its dramatic topography, spicy hotpot, and neon-lit nights. The city sprawls across mountains and rivers, with buildings stacked on hillsides and connected by cable cars.

## Physical Geography
Built at the confluence of the Yangtze and Jialing rivers, Chongqing is one of China's 'Three Furnaces' with sweltering summers. The mountainous terrain creates a unique vertical cityscape with extreme elevation changes.

## Political Context
Chongqing is a direct-controlled municipality, the largest by area in China. It serves as the economic center of western China and a key node in the Belt and Road Initiative.

## People & Culture
With 32 million people in the greater municipality, Chongqing is known for its spicy food culture and resilient, straightforward residents. The city has a strong working-class identity and vibrant street life.
