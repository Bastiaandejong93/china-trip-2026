## Details
Wuhan, divided by the Yangtze and Han rivers, is a city of bridges and lakes. Famous for its Yellow Crane Tower and the springtime cherry blossoms at East Lake.

## Physical Geography
Located in central China at the confluence of the Yangtze and Han rivers, Wuhan experiences hot, humid summers and cold, damp winters. The city is built across three districts separated by water, connected by numerous bridges.

## Political Context
Wuhan is the capital of Hubei Province and a major transportation hub known as the 'thoroughfare of nine provinces.' It's a key center for education, research, and heavy industry in central China.

## People & Culture
Home to 12 million people, Wuhan has a large student population with over 1 million university students. The city has a reputation for straightforward, direct communication and a love of spicy breakfast foods.
